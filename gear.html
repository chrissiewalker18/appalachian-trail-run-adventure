What Do We Need to Bring?

Currently this is just a rough outline to get us thinking about what we will need. Begin thinking about if you need anything new or replaced. Lists will be updated and more detail provided as we get closer to race weekend.

Personal Packing List:

  • Trail Shoes
  • Non-running shoes
  • Head Lamp
  • Water Bottle
  • Sunglasses
  • Running Clothes
  • Warm Clothes for Non-Running (Layers!)
  • Raincoat
  • Personal Running Gear (GPS watch, ipod, etc.)
  • Sleeping bag
  • Towel
  • (Extra socks!)

Group Gear:

  • Tent
  • Food
  • Small First Aid Kit
  • Sunscreen and Bug Spray
  • Camp Chairs
  • Extra flashlights/laterns
