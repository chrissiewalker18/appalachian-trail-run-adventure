Meet Our Team: Happy Trails

We have a team of eight runners who will be running on this exciting adventure together. The members of the team are currently Chrissie, Molly, Danielle, Sean, Christa, Sue, and VJ. Our last runner is still to be determined.

Chrissie

A long time camper and recent runner. Chrissie has done many crazy camping adventures, but none involving running - until now! She is also the team captain and the one to blame for this crazy idea.

Molly

A runnning enthusiast. Molly can most easily be found running through the streets of DC. She has run several half marathons and last year ran her first full marathon. She is always training for yet another race.

Danielle

Danielle will very ready for this race because she is currently training for her first half marathon. She will also probably be the team cheerleader as she is always super enthusiastic!

Sean

Sean has never done a trail run. Or run 6.2 miles on any surface. Or done three runs in one day. But he's just crazy enough to think this is an awesome idea.

Christa

Christa is a seasoned runner who will be joining us from New Jersey. She is probably one of the goofiest, happiest people you'll ever meet. Christa will definitely help keep us entertained and awake over the weekend!

Sue

Sue is an accomplished runner who we're excited to have joining the team. She has lots of race experience. Sue clearly wins the best photo on the team. As the photo might indicate she is married - to VJ!

VJ

VJ enjoys both running and camping. We're excited to have him on the team as he has great knowledge and experience in both areas. He and Sue came as a package deal - we're thrilled we got them both to join us!
