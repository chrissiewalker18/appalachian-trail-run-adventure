Check Out the Trails

The race had three different trails. There are 24 legs of the race. Every runner of the team will do each of the trails once.

The Green Trail:

The green trail is the easiest leg of the race.

The Yellow Trail:

The yellow trail is harder and longer than the green trail. It is roughly the same distance as the red trail, but not quite as difficult.

The Red Trail:

The red trail is the hardest leg of the race. It is a more challenging trail than either the green or yellow.
